Tyredox

TyreDox is the go-to solution for professionals, collectors, and enthusiasts. Enhance your classic and supercar storage by preventing flat-spotting with the ultimate tyre saver!

Category
Shopify
Client
Tyredox
Start Date
September 15, 2024
Designer
Syed Kazmi

Description

During our time as technicians, we saw that customers were regularly replacing expensive tyres, despite little use and often with modest mileage. With less than optimum tyre storage or protection and a potential lack of regular movement, tyres were found to be misshapen, with signs of ovulation and perishing of their structure.

The story

To help optimise your storage, enabling you to enjoy your vehicle at any time without the inconvenience or the worry of additional or unnecessary extra costs. Flat spotted tyres, degraded batteries and pest damage are all factors which might be overlooked when storing classic and prestige vehicles or motorhomes for prolonged periods.

OUR APPROACH

This project made in shopify. For the front end we use HTML, CSS, Js. For the backend we use Liquid Language.
